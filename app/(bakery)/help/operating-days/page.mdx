# Operating Days

The **Operating Days** screen is available to **Admins and Managers**. It lets you configure which days of the week your bakery is open.

## Why this matters

Operating days affect two things:

- **Run-out estimates** on the Inventory screen — the app skips closed days when calculating how long stock will last
- **Schedule display** — closed days can be hidden from the weekly schedule to reduce clutter

## Configuring your days

Toggle each day on or off to match your actual operating schedule. Changes take effect immediately.

## Example

If your bakery is closed Sunday and Monday, disable those days. The run-out estimate on an item like "Croissant" will then skip Sunday and Monday when projecting how many days of stock remain.
