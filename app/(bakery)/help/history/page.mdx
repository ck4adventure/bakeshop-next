# History

The **History** screen shows a complete log of every inventory transaction — batches baked, manual adjustments, and any other stock changes.

## What's recorded

Every entry includes:

- **Item name** — which product was affected
- **Type** — batch bake or manual adjustment
- **Quantity** — how many units were added or removed
- **Reason** — required note for adjustments; automatic for batches
- **Timestamp** — exact date and time of the transaction

## Using history

History is read-only. It's useful for:

- Auditing stock discrepancies
- Reviewing how much was baked on a given day
- Checking who made an adjustment and why

## No deletions

Transactions cannot be deleted. If a batch or adjustment was entered in error, make a correcting adjustment with a clear reason note (e.g. "correcting previous entry — tray count was wrong").
