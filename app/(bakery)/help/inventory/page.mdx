# Inventory

The **Inventory** screen shows your current freezer stock across all items. Use it to get an at-a-glance view of what needs attention, and to make stock adjustments outside of the daily baking flow.

## Filters

At the top of the screen you can filter items by status:

- **All** — every item
- **Critical** — stock is very low (below a critical threshold)
- **Low** — stock is below par
- **Empty** — stock is zero

You can also filter by category using the category chips next to the status filters.

## Reading a card

Each card shows:

- Current **stock count** (large number, top right)
- A **status bar** spanning the card
- **Tomorrow's quota** — how much you need for the next operating day
- A **run-out estimate** — roughly when stock will be exhausted based on the schedule (e.g. "runs out in 2 days")

## Making adjustments

Tap any card to open it. Choose **Adjust Stock** to correct the count:

1. Enter a signed delta — positive to add, negative to remove
2. Provide a reason (required)
3. Tap **Save Adjustment**

All adjustments are logged with timestamp and reason in **History**.
