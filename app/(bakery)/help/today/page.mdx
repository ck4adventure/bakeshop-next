# Today

The **Today** screen is your daily operations dashboard. It shows each item's bake-off target for the current day, lets you log completed batches, and tracks your running freezer stock as you bake.

## What you'll see

Each item card shows:

- **Item name** and current freezer count
- A **status bar** indicating whether stock is above par, low, critical, or empty
- **Today's quota** — how many batches are scheduled for today (from the weekly schedule, or a daily override if one exists)
- **Tomorrow's quota** — a quick glance at what's coming up

## Logging a batch

1. Tap any item card to open it
2. Enter the number of batches you baked (defaults to the item's batch quantity)
3. Tap **Save Batch** — the freezer count updates immediately

## Adjusting stock

If the freezer count is wrong (dropped tray, miscounted, transferred stock), use **Adjust Stock**:

1. Tap an item card and switch to the **Adjust Stock** tab
2. Enter a positive or negative change (e.g. `-3` to remove three units)
3. Add a required **reason** note
4. Tap **Save Adjustment**

All adjustments are recorded in History so nothing is lost.

## Status colors

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Green | Stock is at or above par |
| Yellow | Stock is below par — consider baking soon |
| Red | Stock is critically low |
| Grey | Stock is zero |
