# Settings

The **Settings** screen is available to **Admins**. It controls basic identity information for your bakery.

## Bakery name

The name displayed in the app header. Update it here if your bakery is renamed or rebranded.

## Bakery slug

A short URL-safe identifier used internally (e.g. `sunshine-bakes`). Changing this will affect any bookmarked or shared links, so update with care.

## Saving changes

Tap **Save** after making changes. Settings take effect immediately — a page reload may be needed to see the updated name in the header.
